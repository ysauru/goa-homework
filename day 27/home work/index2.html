About Facebook

History

Facebook was founded in 2004 by Mark Zuckerberg and his college roommates at Harvard University.

Features

Facebook allows users to connect with friends, share photos and videos, join groups, and follow pages.

Statistics

As of 2023, Facebook has over 2.9 billion monthly active users worldwide.

Company Information

Facebook is now part of Meta Platforms, Inc., which also owns Instagram, WhatsApp, and other services.
