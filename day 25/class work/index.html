About Rock Music

Rock emerged in the 1950s, blending blues, jazz, and country. It's known for electric guitars, strong rhythms, and rebellious lyrics.

Subgenres:

  • Classic Rock (Led Zeppelin, The Rolling Stones)
  • Punk Rock (The Clash, Ramones)
  • Heavy Metal (Black Sabbath, Metallica)

Iconic Rock Songs

  1. Bohemian Rhapsody - Queen
  2. Stairway to Heaven - Led Zeppelin
  3. Smells Like Teen Spirit - Nirvana
