about cheetahs

Cheetahs are large cats native to Africa and central Iran. They are known for their incredible speed, reaching up to 70 mph (112 km/h) in short bursts covering distances up to 500 meters.

Key facts:

  • Scientific name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average lifespan: 10-12 years in the wild
  • Weight: 46-159 lbs (21-72 kg)
  • Distinctive black "tear marks" run from eyes to mouth

cheetah speed

cheetahs are built for speed with

  • lightweight body
  • long legs
  • flexible spine
  • long tail for balance

They can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3 seconds - faster than most sports cars!
