our product: Smart Key Finder Tag

Key features:

Bluetooth & Smartphone Tracking Pair with a mobile app to ring the tag when an item is lost nearby (range: 50-100 ft). App shows the last known location on a map. Loud Buzzer Alarm Press a button in the app to make the tag emit a loud beep (helpful for finding items under couch cushions or in bags). Two-Way Finding Press a button on the tag to make the phone ring (helpful when the phone is misplaced). Slim & Durable Design Water-resistant casing (survives rain or spills). Attaches via keyring, adhesive, or clip-on. Long Battery Life Uses a replaceable coin-cell battery (lasts 6-12 months). Optional Premium Features (For Advanced Models) GPS + Cellular Tracking (for real-time location outside Bluetooth range). Crowd-Finding Network (other users’ phones help locate lost items). Voice Assistant Integration (e.g., "Hey Siri, find my keys").

why should YOU buy it

Solves a daily frustration – No more wasted time searching for lost items. Affordable & simple – Cheaper than buying new keys/wallets. Great gift idea – Useful for forgetful friends, elderly parents, or travelers.
